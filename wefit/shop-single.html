Product info & Care

The BH Fitness G2375 Elliptical is a highly customisable cross-trainer that promises to make your workout regime more organised and easy to monitor and at the same time, customised to your specific needs.

Free Delivery

Free Installation

Free on site demo

If you are looking for a rigorous training regime at home, the BH Fitness G2375 Elliptical is the answer to all your queries. The cross-trainer comes with 12 pre-set training profiles and 16 different intensity levels, 4 Heart Rate Control Programs to target cardiovascular zones, Steady Watts Rate Program (SWR: 10-350 watts) especially designed for rehabilitation purposes, integrated Body Fat test program, Recovery test (RT) as well as 5 Custom courses (uPrg). Besides, its elliptical movement allows non-impact cardiovascular exercise to more than 200 muscles. The BH Fitness G2375 Elliptical also comes with magnetic brake system for quiet and smooth pedaling through a range of tensions and a dual backlit monitor to make your workout focused and efficient.

Features and Benefits

  • Magnetic brake system for smooth pedaling through an extensive range of tensions
  • Dual backlit monitor with 12 pre-set training profiles, 16 different intensity levels, 4 HRC
  • Steady Watts Rate Program (SWR: 10-350 watts) for rehabilitation purposes
  • Integrated Body Fat test program, Recovery test (RT) and 5 Custom courses (uPrg)
  • Contact pulse measurement system
  • Wheels for easy transportation
  • Recommended maximum user weight: 115Kg
